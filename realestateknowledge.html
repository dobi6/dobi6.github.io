Here are some tips to be successful in real estate:

  1. Have a job with regular income for 24 months
  2. Look at different types of loans; such as, FHA loan, VA loan, Conventional loan, Confortable loan, Refinance loan, ect...
  3. Pick the loan that best suits your financial needs. (Remeber, a fixed rate mortgage loan of 15-30 years is going to have smaller monthly payemt with a higher interest but a short-term mortgage is going to have high monthly payments but a low interest payment.
  4. Apply for loan that you need, and explain exactly how the property will become successful in the long-term. (You can also tell by the GRM, ROI, NOI and Cap Rate to see wha the properties value will be in the future.
  5. Do tons of research and tour the property before you purchase it.
  6. Make sure there's no proeprty damage to the house before you buy it, if there is consult with the agent or broker.
  7. When selling a property, make sure that you get at least a 10% ROI.
